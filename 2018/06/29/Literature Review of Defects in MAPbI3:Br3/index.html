Literature Review of Defects in MAPbI3/Br3

#Literature Review OIHP#
Density Functional Calculations of Native Defects in CH3NH3PbI3: Effects of Spin−Orbit Coupling and Self-Interaction Error
Author: Mao-Hua Du
Year: 2015
Defect: V_I, I_i, I_MA, Pb_i, Pb_MA
Method: GGA_noSOC, GGA_SOC, HSE_SOC
Phase: beta
Super cell: 2x2x1 (16 formal units)
Main Results:

  • GGA-noSOC raises both VBM and CBM
  • Only iodine and its complexes (I_MA) induce deep electron and hole trapping levels inside the bandgap
    Shallow halogen vacancies in halide optoelectronic materials
    Author: Hongliang Shi and Mao-Hua Du
    Year: 2014
    Defect: Halogen vacancy
    Method: GGA_noSOC, HSE_SOC
    Materials: CH3NH3PbI3,CH3NH3SnI3, CsPbBr3
    Main Results:
  • All have shallow halogen vacancies
  • Insufficient orbital hybridization around the vacancy due to large cation-cation distances and low anion coordination numbers and those with crystal symmetry that prevents strong hybridization of cation dangling bond orbitals.
